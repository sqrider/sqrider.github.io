Experience

Software Intern at Leidos

Led a supervised research effort to develop a secure, cryptographic firmware update turret system for Internet of Things devices. We designed a prototype for an OTA system utilizing TLS/SSL libraries on RTOS devices. I also did basic penetration testing using Kali Linux's tools.

Students for the Exploration and Development of Space

SEDS@UCSD is the first undergraduate organization to design, build, and test a 3D printed metal rocket. I work on the Moonshot Alpha project, a NASA-sponsored initiative to send a 6 unit CubeSat to the moon. I'm on the astrodynamics team, where we use NASA's GMAT and SPICE to calculate trajectories and lunar orbits.

SDHacks 2015

You See SD

Although I was an organizer, I also took part of SDHacks 2015. During the hackathon, we made You See SD, a location sharing application that creates a heat map of densely populated area of UCSD using cell phone data.

HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, MySQL, Google Maps API

RePill

Led a team that designed a concept for a device that improves seniors' quality of life for a design competition sponsored by UCSD's ECE department. RePill notifies the user when to take a variety of pills throughout the day, lessening the complex medicine schedule some seniors have.

RePill was developed using human-centric design, which focuses on empathizing and understanding the client.
